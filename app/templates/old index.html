An Automation to Reduce Traffic Accidents Using Machine Learning

The study of Influencing factors of traffic accidentsis an important research direction in the field of traffic safety. In this project, we built a machine learning model that predicts the probability of RTAs in different locations in London., The strong rules hidden inthese frequent item sets often uncover the association betweeninfluencing factors of accidents, which can be used to reduce theoccurrence of accidents by breaking them. The rules can also beused to probe usual scenes of accidents, and some correspondingsecurity improvement measures can be taken to prevent theaccidents, and ultimately improve the city's traffic safety level.General speaking, association rule mining can produce tons ofweak rules, the study first designed a method to calculate minimalSupport value of training parameters, and further put forward away to extract strong rules automatically. The results of theexperiments showed that these methods proposed in the paper areeffective. Therefore, an automatic modeling algorithm usingassociation rules was finally established to promote the effectiveapplication of association rule mining on intelligent transportation system.
